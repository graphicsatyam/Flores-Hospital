OUR SERVICES

We provide a wide range of services including prevention, screening, assessment and treatment. Our Specialists, Nursing & Support staffs aim to provide each patient with the highest standards of care and exceptional service. We have developed robust Specialty Programs/Centre of Excellence to deliver the high class treatments and the best possible experience for every patient.

  • GENERAL MEDICINE

    • The department of General Medicine deals with the prevention, diagnosis and non-surgical treatment of various diseases. It serves as the first point of contact whenever a patient.

  • HEART AND VASCULAR

    • A skilled staff, state-of-the-art diagnostic technology and leading edge surgical and non-invasive capabilities make MG Hospital a regional destination for heart and vascular care.

  • NEUROSCIENCES

    • Flores Hospital offers innovative treatments and compassionate care for a wide range of neurological diseases, disorders and injuries.

  • ORTHOPAEDICS (BONES AND JOINTS)

    • If a bone or joint problem is slowing you down, Flores Hospital has a complete range of specialized orthopaedic care to help get you back in motion.

  • OBSTETRICS/GYNECOLOGY

    • Obstetrics and gynecology, or OB/GYN for short, doctors receive specialty training in caring for a woman’s complete healthcare needs. Their primary focus is on gynecological care and support during pregnancy and childbirth.

  • DIALYSIS PEDIATRICS

    • From newborns through the teen years, children have medical needs specific to their growing bodies. Pediatricians receive specialty training in caring for infants.

  • DENTISTRY

    • It is said that the mouth is the gateway to the body and hence good oral health forms the foundation for a healthy body. The hospital provides a comprehensive range of dental services right from full mouth digital x-rays, root canal treatment, cosmetic dentistry to fixed tooth implants and many other latest procedures.

  • PULMONOLOGY & SLEEP MANAGEMENT

    • Pulmonology is a medical specialty that deals with diseases involving the respiratory tract. The Department of Pulmonology is committed to providing specialized treatments and excellent comprehensive care to our patients.

  • ENT

    • ENT Department is committed to providing specialized treatments and excellent comprehensive care to our patients. The Department is well equipped with State of the Art lab facilities and Specialists for treatment of patients with diseases and disorders of the ear, nose, throat (ENT), and related structures of the head and neck.

  • COSMETOLOGY & TRAUMA SERVICES

    • Cosmetology is the study and application of beauty treatment. Branches of specialty include hairstyling, skin care, cosmetics, manicures/pedicures, non permanent hair removal such as waxing and sugaring and permanent hair removal processes such as electrology and intense pulsed light(IPL).

  • ENDOCRINOLOGY

    • Endocrinology covers areas including endocrine disease, metabolic disease, diabetes and nutrition. In general, endocrinologists treat diseases that are related to hormone issues. Endocrine diseases fall into broad categories of hormone over or under production, altered tissue response to hormones, or tumours arising from Endocrine tissue.

  • ONCOLOGY

    • Oncology is the branch of medicine that researches, identifies and treats cancer. A physician who works in the field of oncology is an oncologist. Flores offers speciality treatment for cancer patients.

  • OPHTHALMOLOGY

    • Ophthalmology is the area in medicine that deals with the anatomy and physiology of the eyes and diseases related to the eyes. An ophthalmologist specializes in surgical and nonsurgical treatments for the eyes.

  • PEDIATRICS

    • Pediatric surgery is a subspecialty that involves the surgery of fetuses, infants, children, adolescents, and young adults. Common pediatric diseases.

  • UROLOGY

    • Urologists at Flores Hospital provide care for both male and female patients impacted by problems with their urinary tract. We also care for diseases those impact men’s reproductive organs.

  • DERMATOLOGY

    • The Department of Dermatology excels at carrying out complete dermatological care for both common and rare problems of skin, hair, nails and mucous membranes. Flores offers a full spectrum of clinical care for patients of all ages, ensuring that patients receive the most advanced treatments available.
