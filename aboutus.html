OUR HOSPITAL

Today, the city of Ghaziabad, with a population of over 45.50 lacs (apprtox.) has become one of the most crowded city in india. There is an exponential increase in environmental pollution and occupational health hazards due to rapid industrialization. The food stuff are largely adulterated, drinking water supply becoming scarce and unsafe for consumption and lastly industries and automobiles have increased the atmospheric air pollution to an alarmingly unsafe levels. This has led to steep fall in health standards of people.

There is a need for an early and accurate diagnosis and proper treatment of diseases especially after the growing awareness of sound health in the mind of the public. Looking into the scenario and the prospects of providing good health care to people Dr. Manoj Kumar Singh & Dr. Gaurav Gupta have come together to form a company Mridula Healthcare Private Limited (MHPL) which was incorporated as a Private Limited Company during Feb 2011 through which they are keen to provide medical care to the common people. Both the directors are already quite experienced in their respective fields and are already running their practices quite successfully.

At Flores Hospital, we do everything possible to provide the highest level of care to our patients. We strive for excellence in quality and safety, and development of state-of-the-art treatments and technologies; the underlying purpose is to help the patients live a happy and healthy life.

We undertake robust research programs to improve our existing levels of strengths and technology. The hospital is located at one of the best location on the link road connecting NH-24 to NH-58 with open plots on two sides surrounded by lush greenery and nearby residential area, upcoming Siddharth Vihar. It is well connected to Purana Bus Adda, Ghaziabad an upcoming metro station along with all basic facilities in the area.
