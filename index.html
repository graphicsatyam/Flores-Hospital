Our Facilities

24 Hrs. EMERGENCY & TRAUMA

Flores Hospital is a multi-disciplinary super specialty 100 Bed Hospital with modern medical & trauma care facilities and state-of-the-art medical equipment available round the clock to tackle any emergency situation. The Emergency and Trauma Centre is ideally positioned to offer emergency services of the highest calibre.

LAB

24 HOUR Laboratory services are available for in-patients and Out-Patients. The Laboratory at Flores Hospital is committed to accuracy and efficiency. The laboratory has the following pathology services viz. Haematology, Serology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Histopathology and Cytology.

RADIOLOGY

Our Radiology Department has latest and advanced imaging equipped with modern diagnostic facilities and caters to the needs of both inpatients and outpatients, as well as to referrals from outside the Hospital for X-Rays, Ultrasound, CT & MRI Scans.

PHARAMACY

Our Pharmacy is open 24 Hrs. which helps in procuring quality and dispensing them to the outdoor and indoor patients. The main objective of the Pharmacy is to provide genuine medicines at affordable prices to all patients when needed.

ICU

The Department of Critical Care Medicine provides comprehensive medical management for individuals with critical illness or post- surgery treatment. These patients require advanced monitoring and care with modern medical equipment.

24X7 BLOOD BANK

Flores Hospital has one of the finest blood transfusion centres which conducts camp, collects blood and promotes blood donation. Our Blood Bank ensures the critical provisioning of high quality of safe blood and its components to on 24x7 basis for life-saving.

PHYSIOTHERAPY

Physiotherapy helps to develop, maintain and restore maximum movement and functional ability throughout the lifespan. Our Physiotherapy services are available to IPD as well as OPD patients; includes management with specific exercises, manual therapy techniques, and electrotherapeutic modalities.

AYURVEDA

Ayurveda is the ancient Indian holistic medical science which deals with the body, mind and soul in health and disease. It is based on the principles of Panch-Mahabhoota (Space, Air, Fire, Water and Earth) and Tri-Dosha (3 biological humors- Vata, Pitta and Kapha) theories and aims at promotion of positive health, prevention and cure of diseases.

HOMOEOPATHY

Homoeopathy is an alternative method of treatment, based on the nature's Law of Cure, namely 'Like Cures Like'. Homeopathy is the revolutionary and natural medical science. Homoeopathy is gentle and effective system of medicine. The remedies are prepared from natural substances to precise standards and work by stimulating the body's own healing power.
